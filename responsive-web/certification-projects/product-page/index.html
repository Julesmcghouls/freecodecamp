Ghosts Can't Draw

The ghost does the doodling, you do the guessing.

  • Spooky fun for everyone: A Halloween party game that takes only seconds to learn but will have you laughing for hours.

  • 150+ words: Endlessly replayable with a wide variety of topic cards, this fun board game makes a great addition to any family game night.

  • Easy to learn, quick to play: No drawing skills required! The perfect family game for adults and kids aged 8 and up.
