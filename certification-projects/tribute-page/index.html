Kansas Jayhawks

Six National Championship banners hang in the rafters of the historic Allen Fieldhouse at the University of Kansas
James Naismith’s 1891 original rules of basketball are enshrined at the University of Kansas

The Kansas Jayhawks are one of the nation's most celebrated teams in NCAA basketball history, with a tradition of excellence spanning over a century. They have won multiple championships and have produced countless NBA stars. Their iconic mascot, the Jayhawk, symbolizes grit, teamwork, and the spirit of competition.

Major Achievements

  • 1988 NCAA Championship Win
  • Multiple Big 12 Conference Championships
  • Countless NBA Draft Picks
