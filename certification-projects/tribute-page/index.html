About Dog Mascots

Dog mascots are an integral part of many college traditions. They symbolize traits like loyalty, courage, and strength. Whether it's Georgia's UGA or Washington's Dubs, these pups inspire teams and bring fans together.

Our Top Picks

  • University of Georgia: UGA XI, the English Bulldog, represents Georgia's tenacity.
  • Texas A&M: Reveille, the Rough Collie, known as the First Lady of Aggieland.
  • Yale University: Handsome Dan, the first-ever college dog mascot.
  • University of Washington: Dubs II, the majestic Alaskan Malamute.
  • Mississippi State: Jak, an English Bulldog with a cuddly side!
